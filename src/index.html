About me

I am an Assistant developer at Dentsu International’s CRO department. Last years Website and Web Apps development masters student at UOC.

During the day I build A/B tests for international clients, work together with amazingly talented developers, and learn the principals of sitespeed, performance and accessibility.

In the evenings and during the weekend you´ll find me working on exciting university projects involving libraries and technologies such as Angular, Laravel, Sass or Parcel, amongst many others.

What I love the most about being a developer is the fact that there is always a new challenge around the corner, always something new to learn, and a big community of equially enthusiastic people to share your learning experience with.

On my free time I like to relax listening to music, playing bass guitar, and sometimes mixing cocktails for friends and family.
